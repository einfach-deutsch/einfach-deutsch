Einfach Deutsch

Who I am

My name is Isra and I'm from Mexico City. German culture has always fascinated me. That's why I've been living in Germany for over four years now, during which I've been studying German as a Foreign Language. I have a C1 level and know all the challenges of learning German. Moreover, I am familiar with the difficulties of German everyday life as a foreigner. Additionally, thanks to my University career and my years of experience teaching German, I have a large repertoire of methods and strategies to help you learn faster.


My classes

In Einfach Deutsch I offer you online one-on-one lessons. My classes are interactive and individually tailored, taking into account your personality to find the method that works best for you. You will receive all the material you need - Books and charts, but also authentic material like videos, songs or articles. Furthermore, there will be plenty of opportunity to practice your newly acquired skills in conversation to gain fluency.
